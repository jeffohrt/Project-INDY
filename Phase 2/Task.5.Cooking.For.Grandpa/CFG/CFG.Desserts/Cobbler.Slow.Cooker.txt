- grease a slow cooker with 1/2 cup of butter or margarine - leave the rest in the slow cooker
- add 4 cups of frozen berries and 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
- add 1/2 cup of brown sugar
- add 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves
- mix up a box cake mix (white or lemon) and pour batter over the top of berries and spices
- cook on high for 3 to 3 1/2 hrs

Variations
----------

Clan Brown / Mayo liked it this way - Jeff felt it needed twice as much fruit and less sugar.
