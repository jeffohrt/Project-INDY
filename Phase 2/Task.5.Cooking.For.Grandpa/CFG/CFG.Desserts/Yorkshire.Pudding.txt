Yorkshire Pudding

→ 1 cup flour

→ 1 cup milk

→ 2 eggs

Mix everything until smooth and consistent texture.

→ 2 tablespoons butter

→ drippings (roast beef, fried bacon ...)

Grease (mix in drippings if you have them) muffin pan with butter.

Spoon in batter (1 or 2 tablespoons depending on pan size).

→ Bake @ 350 °F (177 °C) for 15 minutes.

Puddings will puff up and then collapse in the center, browning along the edges.
