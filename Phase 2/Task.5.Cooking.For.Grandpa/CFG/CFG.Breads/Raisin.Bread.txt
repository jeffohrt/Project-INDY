Grandma's favorite Raisin Bread

- 1 package or 2 teaspoons of yeast
- 1/4 cup of butter or margarine - melted
- 1/4 cup of warm water
- 1/2 cup of warm milk
- 1 cup of raisins
- 1/4 cup of sugar
- 2 large eggs

Mix all that and let it stand for 20 minutes to get the yeast going.

Mix in 3-4 cups of flour as per white bread.

Finish as per white bread.

If it browns too quickly - cover it with tinfoil.

Variations
----------

You can add other dried fruits and nuts.
You can add a teaspoon or 2 of cinnamon.
